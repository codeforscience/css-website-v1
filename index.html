Code for Science & Society

We empower communities to work together and build innovative technology for the public good.
Research and Scholarship
We work with librarians, researchers, and institutions on improving research reproducibility, research data management, and data preservation.
Government and Society
We work with civic institutions, activists, and government technologists at all levels on developing sustainable systems for public access to public information and facilitate civic engagement.
Journalism and New Media
We work with data journalists, public broadcasters, open source developers, and digital artists to help people share stories, be creative online, and build next generation social media tools.
